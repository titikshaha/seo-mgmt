Guide 01

What is an AI Floor Plan Generator?

An AI floor plan generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to automatically create architectural floor plans from a plain-language description — no CAD software, no drafting experience required. You describe your project (plot size, number of rooms, building type, orientation preferences), and the AI produces a complete, dimensioned 2D floor plan within seconds.

Traditional floor plan creation required an architect or draughtsperson to manually draw every wall, door, window, and room — a process that could take 3–6 hours for a simple residential plan. AI tools like VirtuviAI compress this to under 30 seconds, producing output that includes room labels, dimensions, north indicators, and automatic MEP layout overlays and 3D views.

  • 2D floor plans with accurate room dimensions and labels
  • MEP overlays — electrical, plumbing, HVAC automatically placed
  • 3D walkthrough views from the same brief
  • Regional compliance — Vastu for India, Part M for UK, NCC for Australia
  • Export as PDF, high-res image, or DWG-compatible file

Guide 02

How accurate is a Construction Cost Estimator?

A good construction cost estimator produces a bill of quantities accurate to within 10–15% of actual costs at concept stage — sufficient for budgeting decisions, loan applications, and initial contractor discussions. Builtattic's Vision tool calculates material quantities based on your floor plan area, building type, number of storeys, and construction quality grade, adjusting for regional material pricing across India, UK, US, Canada, and Australia.

The most accurate estimates come from feeding a VirtuviAI floor plan directly into the cost estimator — because the AI already knows the exact room dimensions, wall lengths, and window counts, it calculates quantities with precision rather than relying on generic area-based approximations. Guide 03

Can AI Replace Architects for Floor Plans?

For concept and planning-stage floor plans, AI is already faster and cheaper than manual drafting. For complex, high-stakes, or bespoke projects — AI does not replace an architect's expertise, judgement, and professional accountability. What AI tools like VirtuviAI replace is the repetitive part — producing initial floor plan sketches for client meetings, generating multiple layout options for a site, or creating permit drawings for straightforward residential projects.

If you need a licensed professional, Builtattic's Hire an Architect tool connects you with verified professionals near you — an AI-first approach that still respects the value of human expertise when it matters.

Guide 04

How to Generate a Floor Plan Online

  • Step 1 — Describe your project: Enter plot dimensions, number of floors, rooms, and any regional preferences (Vastu, orientation, accessibility).
  • Step 2 — Generate: VirtuviAI's AI produces a dimensioned 2D floor plan in under 30 seconds with room labels, dimensions, and north indicator.
  • Step 3 — Add MEP and 3D: One click adds electrical, plumbing, and HVAC overlays. Another produces a 3D view for client presentations.
  • Step 4 — Export and share: Download as PDF or DWG-compatible file. Submit to planning authorities or carry through to cost and timeline tools.
